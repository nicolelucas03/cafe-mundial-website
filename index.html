Every week, we serve hot meals and beverages while distributing essential items like non-perishables, warm clothing, and other supplies. Together, with our volunteers, local businesses, and supporters, we create a safe, welcoming space where everyone can feel valued.

In addition to meeting immediate needs, we strive to foster meaningful connections within the community. Through our outreach programs, we offer resources for housing assistance, job placement, and mental health support, empowering individuals to overcome challenges and build a brighter future.

We also host seasonal drives and special events, such as holiday dinners, back-to-school supply giveaways, and wellness workshops, ensuring our impact extends beyond daily essentials. Every effort is guided by our belief in dignity, respect, and compassion for all.

How To Get Involved

Donate

Help us feed our community by contributing non-perishable items or freshly prepared meals! Whether you're an individual, family, or business, your donation makes a difference.

Funds

Every dollar you contribute goes directly toward fighting hunger in our community. With your generosity, we can purchase essential food items, cover operational costs, and expand our reach to serve more families in need.

Volunteer

Volunteering with our food drive is a rewarding way to give back and directly impact the lives of those in need. Whether you have a few hours or want to commit regularly, we have opportunities for everyone!

Funds

Your generous contributions allow us to directly support those in need by purchasing essential items, especially during the colder months.

Funds are used to buy:

  • Fresh food to distribute to individuals and families experiencing food insecurity.
  • Non-perishable goods to ensure no one goes hungry.
  • Winter essentials such as blankets, jackets, gloves, and other cold-weather gear to provide warmth and comfort.

Every dollar helps us make a difference in our community, ensuring that everyone has access to basic necessities and support during challenging times. Venmo us @elcafemundial. Thank you for helping us bring hope and care to those who need it most.
